Balance is a meditative but active state, a state that focuses on maintaining the equilibrium in the mind-body.

Balance Begins, Balance Ends

Balance begins as we are conceived into this life and continues in our early years – innocent, playful, and intuitive. Living for the moment, only for the moment. Nothing less and nothing more, unequivocally yourself – balanced. We did not have to try – we just were. We just did things as we pleased, whenever we pleased, time was never a factor. We did not see behind us, nor in front of us, we just saw what was besides us – present.

We just did things as we pleased, whenever we pleased, time was never a factor. We did not see behind us, nor in front of us, we just saw what was beside us. As we progress through life, we disconnect with this sense, and these moments of balance are few and far between.

Balance is dynamic and quiet - is the act to equipoise between opposing forces to find centeredness. This point is always shifting as new experiences arise, making an individual point of balance inherently different at any moment.

POSE

There is no balance without knowing what it is like to be off-balanced at times. Maybe this is why counterbalance came to be - to resist and bring us back to balance when we have been gone for too long. Even when you disregard it, balance it is always there - spectating from afar, waiting for your inevitable return. Balance ends, so then later balance begins once again.
